What is Sabię?

Sabię is an innovative PropTech platform integrating advanced AI to transform how you rent, buy, and manage property. With state-of-the-art technology, we make real estate transactions secure, efficient, and user-friendly.

How does Sabię differentiate itself in the PropTech market?

Sabię enhances security, improves efficiency, and provides tailored solutions for buyers, sellers, renters, and landlords. Our platform is uniquely designed to simplify complex processes, offer real-time market insights, and support diverse real estate needs, making property dealings more accessible and user-friendly for everyone involved.

What is the expected launch date for Sabię, and how can I get early access?

Sabię is gearing up for a dynamic launch in the coming months. Sign up now on our website to get exclusive early access and stay updated with our development milestones.

What can users expect from Sabię at launch?

At launch, Sabię will offer competitive pricing with clear, value-driven plans tailored to different user needs, ensuring high-quality services at every price point.

What types of properties and transactions can users expect to handle through Sabię?

Users can manage a wide range of properties, from residential rentals and sales to commercial real estate transactions, all facilitated through our AI-driven platform.

Can I manage international properties with Sabię?

Yes, Sabię is designed with global property owbers in mind, featuring multi-currency support, cross-border transaction facilitation, and comprehensive market analytics to decisions with ease.

How does Sabię ensure the security and authenticity of listings?

Our multi-layered verification process includes on-site inspections, documentation reviews, and blockchain technology to certify the authenticity of every listing and protect against fraud.

How does Sabię ensure protection of user data?

Sabię leverages cutting-edge encryption and blockchain technologies to secure user transactions and safeguard sensitive information. Our platform's built-in transparency features include a secure, tamper-proof ledger, offering top-level data protection against breaches. This approach ensures a trusted and secure environment for all your real estate dealings, providing peace of mind with every transaction.
