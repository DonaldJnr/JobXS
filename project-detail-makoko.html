Empowering Communities, Transforming Futures

At Sabię, we are excited to explore an upcoming initiative that holds the promise of transforming lives through sustainable and innovative housing solutions. Project Makoko is currently in the planning stages and symbolizes our forward-thinking approach to addressing housing challenges in unique environments.

Vision for Sustainable Living

Situated in the iconic water-bound community of Lagos, Makoko faces distinct challenges that require equally unique solutions. Our vision for Project Makoko is to create resilient, floating homes that combine modern design with sustainability, tailored specifically to the needs of this vibrant community.

As we join forces with Sabię on Project Makoko, our shared vision of fostering sustainable community development comes to life. This partnership is a testament to our belief that innovative technology and local expertise can together create transformative solutions for communities like Makoko. We are excited about this opportunity to combine MJ Foundation's deep understanding of community needs with Sabie's cutting-edge proptech capabilities, aiming to build not just homes, but a sustainable future for every resident.

Marjana Ali

Founder MJ Foundation

Collaborative Efforts for a Better Tomorrow

We are in the preliminary phases of partnering with the MJ Foundation, renowned for its commitment to environmental sustainability and community empowerment in Nigeria. This collaboration aims to blend Sabię’s technological innovations with the foundation’s grassroots insights to develop a model for community-driven development.

Project Goals

While still in the exploratory phase, our goals for Project Makoko include:

  • Designing modular floating homes using sustainable materials.
  • Implementing smart technologies to enhance energy efficiency and water management.
  • Engaging the Makoko community directly to ensure the solutions meet their real needs and support community empowerment.
